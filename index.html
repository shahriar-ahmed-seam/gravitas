The universe, in a tab

No approximations. Just raw gravity.

Every body pulls on every other across all three axes, integrated with adaptive-substep RK4 and rendered through a photoreal WebGL pipeline.

Black holes & accretion disks

Event horizons, glowing photon rings and 1,500-particle Keplerian accretion disks. Massive bodies bend the paths of everything around them.

🌀

Tidal disruption

Cross the Roche limit and a body shreds into an orbiting ring of debris.

🔥

Tidal heating

Bodies near massive neighbors glow with volcanic, magma-hued surfaces.

Lagrange points

True 3D orbital mechanics
